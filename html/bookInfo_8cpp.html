Displays detailed information about a specific book.

Parameters
arrayArray of bookType objects representing the inventory
bookIndexIndex of the book in the array to display information for

This function displays a formatted view of all information for a specific book, including:

  • Title
  • ISBN
  • Author
  • Publisher
  • Date added
  • Quantity on hand
  • Wholesale price
  • Retail price

The information is presented in a clean, formatted layout with borders for visual clarity. No modifications to the book data are performed by this function.
